THE FAILURE TODAY

The people who need help and the people who can help are visible in different systems.

Flood response breaks at the coordination layer: alerts, resident needs, volunteer capacity, shelter space, routes, and authorizations arrive separately.

  • Medical and mobility needs
  • Transport and accommodation

COMMUNITY CAPACITY“Who needs what I can provide?”
  • Skills and travel radius
  • Available shelter spaces
  • Verification and status
COORDINATOR“What is true, urgent, and authorized?”
  • Conflicting reports
  • Multiple resource matches
  • Audit and accountability
